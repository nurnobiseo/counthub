How Mortgage Calculations Work

Mortgage calculations use the formula for amortizing loans, which calculates fixed monthly payments that gradually pay down the principal while also covering the interest charges. Your monthly payment consists of four components: principal, interest, property taxes, and homeowners insurance. The formula ensures that each payment applies to both interest and principal, with the interest portion decreasing and the principal portion increasing over the life of the loan.
