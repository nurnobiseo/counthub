About Business Days Calculation

Business days typically refer to Monday through Friday, excluding weekends and public holidays. This calculator helps you determine the number of working days between two dates, which is useful for project planning, deadline tracking, and estimating delivery times. The calculation excludes weekends (Saturday and Sunday) and any selected holidays that fall within the date range.
