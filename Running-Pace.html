About Running Pace

Running pace is typically measured in minutes per kilometer or minutes per mile. It represents how long it takes to cover a specific distance. Understanding your pace helps you train effectively, set realistic goals, and track your progress. Different training zones target various physiological adaptations: easy pace builds endurance, tempo pace improves lactate threshold, and interval training increases speed.
