About Credit Card Payoff

Credit card debt can be expensive due to high interest rates. This calculator helps you understand how long it will take to pay off your balance and how much interest you'll pay. The avalanche method focuses on paying off debts with the highest interest rates first, while the snowball method focuses on paying off smallest balances first for psychological wins. Making more than the minimum payment can significantly reduce the time and interest paid.
